About CropAI

Project Overview

CropAI is an AI-powered crop recommendation system that combines machine learning, explainable AI (XAI), and economic viability analysis to help farmers make informed decisions about crop selection.

The system analyzes soil nutrients (N, P, K), climate conditions (temperature, humidity, rainfall), and soil pH to recommend the most suitable crops with confidence scores and detailed explanations.
